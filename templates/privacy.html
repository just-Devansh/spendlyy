Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we also store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling account features like login and profile management.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you through your authenticated account. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently and irreversibly removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the platform, but these providers do not have access to your expense data for their own purposes.

Any third-party services we use are bound by their own privacy policies, and we choose providers that meet reasonable data protection standards.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.

By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
